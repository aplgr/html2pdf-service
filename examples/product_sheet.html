🛡 Safe Usage Guide

  1. Charge only with the supplied charger (5V/3A min.).
  2. Do not expose to water, direct sunlight, or heat above 60°C.
  3. Keep out of reach of children and pets.
  4. Do not open, puncture, or incinerate the device.
  5. Always disconnect after charging – do not leave plugged in unattended.
  6. Store in a cool, dry place (recommended 20°C/68°F).
  7. Discontinue use if swelling, leaking, or strange odor occurs.
